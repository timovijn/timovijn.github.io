About us

Quantodian is a three-men operation and is located in The Netherlands. Our goal is to help investors to navigate the data-driven trading landscape by offering concise and honest information on our trading algorithms.

Investors can benefit in the following ways:

  1. Compare the published results of our strategies to your own strategies.
  2. Get access to our real-time trading signals directly. Contact us for more information.
  3. Rebuild our trading indicators and/or strategies yourself to learn and see how these perform without us telling you.
